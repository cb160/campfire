# Ham and Eggs

Ham, Eggs \
Ham, Eggs \
Ham, Eggs \
We like ours nice and brown \
We like ours upside down \
Ham and Eggs 

## Instructions

 - Divide the group into two sections
 - One section will shout **Ham**, the other will shout **Eggs**
 - Get them the practice the first bit 3 times
    - Ham, Eggs
    - Ham, Eggs
    - Ham, Eggs
- Then add in the following:
    - For the Ham section
       - We like ours nice and brown
    - For the Eggs Section
        - We like ours upside down
    
- Finally finish off with a mexican wave (You need to pick someone in the middle to shout **And**)
   - Haaaammm **And** Eggggssssss
